### About Me
I am **Junteng Liu**, a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.hkust.edu.hk/), supervised by [Professor Junxian He](https://junxianhe.com/). My research focuses on natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML), with a focus on improving the reasoning capabilities, truthfulness, and interpretability of large language models (LLMs) and vision-language models (VLMs).

### Research Interests
My research interests are centered around:
- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Developing methods to synthesize verifiable reasoning data and improve logical reasoning in LLMs using reinforcement learning.
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**: Understanding and mitigating perception bottlenecks in VLMs for chart understanding and other vision-language tasks.
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Exploring universal truthfulness mechanisms and interpretability in LLMs, including identifying hyperplanes related to truthfulness.
